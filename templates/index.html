STOCK MARKET DATA ANALYSIS

Summary of Project


Our project analyzes the relationship between the S&P 500 Index and several factors. The factors we looked into were Disasters (Technological and Natural), CPI, and stock prices of individual companies within 11 sectors. We performed two machine learning analyses using multiple linear regression to predict the S&P 500 Index. Although we have created two multiple linear regression models, there are still many different factors to consider in predicting the S&P 500 Index such as magnitude of disasters (i.e. how many persons killed, etc). We also utilized Tableau and Matplotlib to create multiple visualizations of the data. Natural disaster data was obtained from Emergency Events Database (EM-DAT). Financial data was obtained from Yahoo Finance, Macro Trends, and DataHub.io.
