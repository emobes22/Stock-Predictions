DISASTERS & CPI vs S&P 500 INDEX

MULTIPLE LINEAR REGRESSION

We used sklearn and statsmodels libraries in Jupyter Notebook to run a multiple linear regression on the 31 year (1987-2017) dataset of Natural Disasters & Consumer Price Index vs the S&P 500 Index.The OLS (Ordinary Least Squares) Regression Results image below is the output provided from the statsmodels library. The R-squared value is 0.848 which is close to 1 meaning that the multiple linear regression model is a good fit with our data.


It is important to point out that the Natural Disasters P-Value is greater than 0.05, meaning that there may be no siginificant relationship between this variable and the S&P500 Index.
